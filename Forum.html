Tell us about your experience!

What have you heard or seen about the lack of participation of women in engineering/STEM fields? If you identify as a woman or non-binary, what is your experience working in STEM fields?

This is a safe space for all who share, and is a chance for you to give us your experience :)

World

Tania Towry

March 28

“I had been working at a tech company for two years and had just received a promotion. However, when I received my new offer, I was shocked to see that my salary was only slightly higher than my previous one, despite the added responsibilities. I knew that I had negotiated just as well as my male colleagues, and I couldn't help but feel that my gender had played a role in the pay gap.”



World

Alisha Kalashi

Nov 11

While it was disheartening to experience the pay gap firsthand, I'm proud of the action we took to address it. It's crucial that we continue to speak up and fight for equal pay, not just for ourselves, but for all women in STEM and beyond. This would surely benefit future women in STEM along with other women of color in STEM. I would not want future women to go through the experiences I have dealt with in this field. I wholeheartedly support this organization.
