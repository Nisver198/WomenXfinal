Our Mission

This website is designed as a safe space for women around the world to discuss the prejudices they experience in the workplace and society. Historically women have been stereotyped to tend to home affairs and were rarely part of the workforce. In the past few decades, women have been one of the pillars of success in many of the economy’s industries. Despite their many significant successes, women are still alienated from many workplaces. Discrimination within job opportunities and pay with female workers have been really common throughout the world. Our team hopes to spread positivity and support all women who deserve recognition and equal opportunities for their hard work.

Our Team

Shriya Bhamidipati

As a rising computer science major she’s motivated to raise awareness for the lack of women in STEM fields. Throughout her life, she has noticed that in all the STEM classes she has taken a part in having more men than women. For example, in her school’s computer science class, around 75% of the class is male, only around 25% is female. The same applies in her physics class. She has also noticed a massive pay gap between males and females, even if they do the same job. She firmly believes that such gender bias should not exist, and is not beneficial to our society today.

Nisha Vernekar

An aspiring software engineer interested in dissolving the disparate treatment between men and women in the tech industry. Since middle school she has noticed a lack of women in more STEM related courses which were concerning to her. In many bi companies she has noticed a lack of women in leadership positions and leading big projects. Her goal is to establish a safe and fair space for all women in tech and in other industries. She hopes to inspire young girls to work hard to achieve their dreams of becoming successful in life.

Ananaya Agarwal

As a computer science major, she is passionate about technology and wants to bridge the gender gap in STEM fields. She believes in empowering and supporting women in tech through networking, mentorship programs, and connecting with like-minded individuals to create a more inclusive and diverse tech industry. She is committed to doing their part to make this a reality and excited to be a part of this movement.

Nimrit Dhanjal

With a unique background in both business and physics, and a passion for equality and a drive to make a positive impact, she is committed to helping close the gender pay gap and promote diversity and inclusion in the workplace. Taking on two subjects dominated and often taught by men, she hopes to take charge and inspire women around the globe to take matters into their own hands.
